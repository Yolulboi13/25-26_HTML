Ordered and Unordered List

Unordered lists use the ul and li tags. Ul declares the list type and li is the items.

  • Charmander
  • Squirtle
  • Bulbasaur

Ordered lists use ol instead of ul. They essentially make a numbered list.

Mechanically the weakest Gen 3 starters:
  1. Treeko
  2. Torchick
  3. Mudkip

Descriptive lists use dl, dt, and dd tags. Dl declares the list type, dt is the topic, and dd is the data
